🌍 Understanding the World Cup
⚽ What is the FIFA World Cup?

The FIFA World Cup is the biggest football (soccer) tournament on the planet. It happens every 4 years. In 2026, for the first time ever, it's hosted across 3 countries: the USA, Canada and Mexico — with 48 national teams competing across 104 matches over about 6 weeks.

📅 How the tournament works
1
Group Stage (June 11–27)
48 teams split into 12 groups of 4. Every team plays 3 games. Top 2 from each group advance, plus the 8 best 3rd-place teams — total 32 teams move on.
2
Round of 32 (June 28–July 3)
32 teams play head-to-head knockout matches. Lose and you're out. If it's a draw after 90 min, there's extra time — and if still tied, a penalty shootout decides the winner.
3
Round of 16 (July 4–7)
Also called "Octavos de Final". 16 teams remain. Same knockout format — one game, winner advances.
4
Quarterfinals (July 9–11)
8 teams left. Four matches. The 4 winners go to the semis.
5
Semifinals (July 14–15)
4 teams, 2 matches. Winners go to the Final; losers play each other for 3rd place.
🏆
Final (July 19 — New York/New Jersey)
The last 2 teams standing. One match. One champion.
🎯 What is the Prode?

A Prode (short for "pronóstico deportivo") is a prediction league — you guess the score of each match before it starts, earn points based on how right you were, and compete against your friends on the leaderboard.

You don't need to know football deeply. Even a lucky guess on the exact score earns big points. Anyone can win!

📝 How to play — step by step
1️⃣ Go to the Matches tab. You'll see all upcoming matches.
2️⃣ For each match, type in how many goals you think each team will score and hit Save Prediction.
3️⃣ Predictions lock 1 hour before kickoff — no changing your mind after!
4️⃣ After each match, points are added automatically. Check Ranking to see where you stand.
5️⃣ For knockout matches that end in a draw, pick who you think wins the penalty shootout for a bonus +3 pts.
❓ Common questions
Can I edit my prediction?
Yes, up until 1 hour before the match starts. After that it's locked.
Do I need to predict every match?
No, but you only earn points for matches you've predicted. The more you fill in, the better your chances.
What if a knockout game ends in a draw but I didn't pick penalties?
You still get points for the score and outcome (draw) — just no penalty bonus.
When are results entered?
The admin enters official results after each match. Points update for everyone instantly.
